Free Guy

2021 | 1h 55m | Action, Comedy, Sci-Fi

Free Guy is a 2021 American science fiction action comedy film directed by Shawn Levy from a screenplay by Matt Lieberman and Zak Penn and a story by Lieberman. The film stars Ryan Reynolds, Jodie Comer, Lil Rel Howery, Utkarsh Ambudkar, Joe Keery, and Taika Waititi. It follows a bank teller who discovers he is a non-player character in an open-world video game and becomes the hero of the story. The film was produced by 21 Laps Entertainment and Maximum Effort, and distributed by 20th Century Studios.
